About Me

Hello, my name is Jiawei Xing. I'm currently a PhD candidate at The Ohio State University. In Dr. Igor Jouline lab, I use computational methods to study protein structures and evolution. My current research topic focuses on the most common sensor, Per-Arnt-Sim (PAS) domain, in signal transduction systems.

With a background in biology and an interest in computer science, I'm passionate about combining computer and biology together to better understand the secret of life sciences.
