About Me

Hello, I'm Jiawei Xing, a computational biologist. I'm currently a postdoc in the Siepel lab at Cold Spring Harbor Laboratory. Before that, I received my PhD degree from the Jouline lab at The Ohio State University. My research interests are computational and evolutionary biology.
