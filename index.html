About Me

Hello, my name is Jiawei Xing. I'm currently a PhD candidate at The Ohio State University. In Jouline lab, I use computational methods to study protein structures and evolution. My current research topic focuses on protein sensors in signal transduction systems.

With a background in biology and an interest in computer science, I'm passionate about combining computer and biology together to better understand the secret of life sciences.
